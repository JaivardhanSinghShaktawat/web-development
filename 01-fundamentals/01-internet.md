# Internet

## What is Internet ?

It is a global network where multiple devices like laptop , computer etc are connected to each other via optic fibre cables/physical devices across the entire world and through routers and switches in the entire country or cities.
Computers use **TCP/IP protocol** to communicate with each other.

The combination of different local area networks together is called as internet.

The signal travel at the speed of light.So whenever we search something the
signal travels at the speed of light through optic fibre cables and we get the results in milliseconds.

## Need Of Internet

1. To communicate with other devices.
2. To send data.
