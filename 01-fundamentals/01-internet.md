# Internet

## What is Internet ?

The combination of different local area networks together is called as internet.

Go to submarinecablesmap website to see the different cables that creates internet.

Note : All the devices are connected with each other through optic fibre cables across the entire word and in countries different cities and states are connected via wires and cables.

## Need Of Internet

1. To communicate with other devices.
2. To send data.
