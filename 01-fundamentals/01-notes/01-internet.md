# Internet

## What is Internet ?

The combination of different local area networks together is called as internet.

Go to submarinecablemap website to see the different cables that creates internet.

**Note :** All the devices are connected with each other through optic fibre cables across the entire word and in countries different cities and states are connected via wires and cables.The data travels very fast through these cables and we get the result very fast.

Our mobile devices are connected with the towers and towers has cables that are present underground and data flows through those underground cables.

## Need Of Internet

1. To communicate with other devices.
2. To send data.
